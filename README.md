# 🏠 NIVAS 2.0 – Student Hostel & Accommodation Finder

NIVAS 2.0 is a full-stack web application that helps students find suitable hostels and rental rooms near engineering colleges. The platform simplifies the accommodation search process by providing verified listings, essential details, and an easy-to-use interface for students relocating for higher education.

---

## 🚀 Live Demo

🌐 **Website:** https://nivas-accommodations.vercel.app/NIVAS-2.O/

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

Every year, thousands of students joining engineering colleges struggle to find reliable hostels and rental rooms while simultaneously managing the admission process. Due to limited time, lack of reliable information, and decision-making pressure, students and parents often end up choosing from only a small number of hostels, even though many better options are available.

**NIVAS 2.0** solves this real-world problem by providing a centralized platform where students can easily discover nearby hostels and rental rooms before joining college.

---

## 🌍 Real-World Impact

- Successfully used by **2026 EAMCET-qualified students** joining **SRKR Engineering College** and **Vishnu Institute of Technology**.
- Features **15+ verified hostel listings** with accommodation details.
- Helps students and parents reduce the time and effort required to search for accommodation during the admission period.
- Makes the hostel selection process more informed, convenient, and stress-free.
